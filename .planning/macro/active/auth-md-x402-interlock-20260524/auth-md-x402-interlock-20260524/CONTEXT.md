## Product Canon

The controlling posture is:

- Handshake is protected action infrastructure for automated decision making.
- x402 is the first official buyer-side `x402_payment.exact` per-call protected
  spend wedge.
- auth.md is credential provenance, custody, claim, revocation, scope,
  audience, and lifecycle evidence until an exact protected action is
  gateway-checked.
- `.planning/` is planning evidence, not product truth, unless separately
  promoted and validated.

## Synthesis Consensus

All raw lenses converged on the same boundary:

- keep x402 as the official narrow executable wedge;
- keep auth.md closed as adapter/provenance/custody evidence;
- combine the two first as a redacted evidence/read packet;
- defer any composite execution action until packet tests and hostile
  validation prove one exact contract, one-use authority, gateway ordering,
  redaction, isolation, and replay refusal.

## Chair Choice

Use a packet profile, not a fused adapter or new market category:

```text
auth_md_x402_interlock_packet.v0
profile: protected_spend_provenance.v0
```

The packet can correlate evidence, refs, digests, refusals, proof gaps, and
non-claims. It cannot create policy, greenlights, gateway checks, credentials,
signatures, mutations, receipts, terminal certificates, settlement, or trust.

## Important Boundaries

- auth.md discovery, registration, ID-JAG, claim, revocation, scope, and
  audience are upstream evidence only.
- x402 challenge, selected requirement, signer availability, payment payload,
  signed retry, and downstream response are not policy, greenlight, settlement,
  or service success.
- Runtime, MCP, CLI, SDK, reports, demo output, hosted readiness, review, and
  packet readbacks are proposal/evidence/read surfaces only.
- Gateway custody and gateway signing happen only after `VerifiedGatewayCheck`.

## Planned Evidence Shape

The local/reference packet should reconstruct:

- generated attempt refs;
- auth.md PRM, authorization-server metadata, `agent_auth`, registration,
  claim, revocation, audience, scope, credential-ref, and isolation evidence;
- x402 challenge, selected requirement, amount, payee, network, token, endpoint,
  request digest, signer custody, and signed retry evidence;
- exact action contract digest;
- policy decision and one-use greenlight;
- gateway check and one-use consumption;
- post-gate credential resolution evidence;
- post-gate payment signature evidence;
- downstream observation, refusal, replay refusal, proof gap, and non-claims;
- redaction manifest and blocked checks.

## Current Capability Boundary

The macro plan is a planning package. It does not prove live provider custody,
hosted operation, WorkOS/auth.md endorsement, x402 settlement, seller
middleware, facilitator operation, aggregate budgets, cross-org trust, package
safety, or broad runtime containment.
